# Go Outside Platform - Demo Recording Guide

**Target Duration**: 8-10 minutes  
**Audience**: Potential users, stakeholders, investors

---

## Pre-Recording Setup

### Before You Start

1. **Clear Browser Cache** - Fresh start for clean demo
2. **Prepare Test Accounts**:
   - Customer account (or register new one)
   - Venue Owner account
   - Admin account (admin@gooutside.com / admin123)
3. **Have Test Data Ready**:
   - At least 2-3 venues visible on homepage
   - One booking in progress
   - Sample reviews
4. **Close Unnecessary Tabs** - Clean browser window
5. **Set Browser Zoom** - 100% for best recording quality
6. **Test Audio** - Ensure microphone is working

### Recording Tips

- **Speak clearly** and at a moderate pace
- **Pause briefly** after each action for clarity
- **Highlight key features** with mouse movements
- **Use smooth scrolling** - avoid jerky movements
- **Show, don't just tell** - demonstrate features visually

## Demo Script (8-10 Minutes)

### Introduction (30 seconds)

**What to Say:**
> "Welcome to Go Outside, a comprehensive venue and activity booking platform designed for Ghana. Today I'll show you how customers discover and book venues, how owners manage their listings, and how administrators oversee the platform. Let's get started!"

**What to Show:**
- Homepage overview
- Clean, modern interface
- Navigation bar

---

### Part 1: Customer Journey - Discovery (2 minutes)

#### 1.1 Homepage Overview (30 seconds)

**What to Say:**
> "This is our homepage. Notice the clean, modern design with dark theme and orange accents. Users can immediately see featured venues and activities, search by location, and browse by category."

**What to Show:**
- Hero section with search bar
- Featured venues grid
- Category filters
- "Happening Soon" activities section
- Navigation bar (Discover, Venues, Activities, Become a Host)

**Actions:**
- Scroll through featured venues
- Hover over a venue card to show interaction
- Point out the search bar

#### 1.2 Search & Filter (45 seconds)

**What to Say:**
> "Let's search for a restaurant in Accra. Users can filter by category, location, price range, and capacity. The search is instant and responsive."

**What to Show:**
- Click on "Venues" in navigation
- Show filter sidebar
- Select "Restaurants" category
- Select location (e.g., "Accra")
- Show filtered results
- Click on a venue card

**Actions:**
- Use category filter
- Use location filter
- Show results updating
- Click to view venue details

#### 1.3 Venue Detail Page (45 seconds)

**What to Say:**
> "Here's a detailed venue page. Users can see photos, description, location on a map, verified reviews, pricing, and availability. Notice the verified badge on reviews - only guests who actually attended can leave reviews."

**What to Show:**
- Photo gallery (if available)
- Venue description
- Location map
- Reviews section with verified badges
- Pricing information
- "Book Now" button
- Save button (if logged in)

**Actions:**
- Scroll through photos
- Show map location
- Scroll to reviews
- Point out verified badges
- Show pricing details

### Part 2: Customer Journey - Booking (2.5 minutes)

#### 2.1 Login/Registration (30 seconds)

**What to Say:**
> "To book a venue, users need to create an account. The registration process is simple and supports both customers and venue owners."

**What to Show:**
- Click "Menu" button (or navigate to login)
- Show login page
- Click "Sign up" or show registration
- Show role selection (Customer/Owner)
- Fill in form fields (quickly, don't complete)

**Actions:**
- Navigate to login page
- Show registration form
- Highlight role selection

#### 2.2 Booking Flow (2 minutes)

**What to Say:**
> "Once logged in, let's book this venue. The booking process is a simple multi-step form where users select date, time, number of guests, and any special requirements."

**What to Show:**
- Navigate back to venue detail page
- Click "Book Now" button
- Show booking form:
  - Date picker
  - Time selection
  - Guest count
  - Special requirements
- Show sidebar with booking summary
- Progress indicator (Step 1, 2, 3)
- Fill in booking details
- Show payment options (Paystack)
- Click "Continue to Payment"

**Actions:**
- Select a date
- Select time slots
- Enter guest count
- Add special requirements
- Show booking summary updating
- Navigate to payment step

**What to Say (continued):**
> "The platform integrates with Paystack for secure payments. Users can pay a deposit to secure their booking. The payment is held in escrow until the event is completed."

**What to Show:**
- Payment form
- Paystack payment button
- Show secure payment notice
- **Note**: Don't complete actual payment (just show the flow)

**Actions:**
- Show payment form
- Point out Paystack integration
- Show escrow notice
- Explain you won't complete payment for demo

## Troubleshooting During Demo

### If Something Doesn't Work:

1. **Payment Flow**: 
   - "The payment integration is fully functional. For this demo, I'm showing the flow without completing the actual transaction."

2. **Slow Loading**:
   - "The platform is optimized for performance. In production with proper hosting, load times are much faster."

3. **Missing Data**:
   - "This is a demo environment. In production, you'd see many more venues and activities."

4. **Errors**:
   - Have backup screenshots ready
   - Skip to next section smoothly
   - Don't apologize excessively
